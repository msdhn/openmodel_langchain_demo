# AML Policy

## Purpose
Defines controls to detect, prevent, and report money laundering and related financial crime risks.

## Core Controls
- Customer risk assessment and segmentation
- Transaction monitoring and alert management
- Sanctions and watchlist screening

## Alert Handling
- Triage alerts by risk severity
- Escalate suspicious activity for investigation
- Maintain case notes and decision audit trail

## Reporting
- Report suspicious activity to relevant authorities as required
- Follow legal timelines and confidentiality obligations

## Training and Governance
- Mandatory AML training for relevant staff
- Periodic testing of AML control effectiveness
