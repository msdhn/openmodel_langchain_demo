# Sustainable Banking Policy

## Purpose
Outlines how sustainability principles are integrated into lending, investment, and operations.

## Strategic Objectives
- Reduce financed emissions over time
- Increase support for green transition projects
- Improve transparency in sustainability reporting

## Lending Guidance
- ESG risk considered in credit decisions
- Sector-specific restrictions for high-impact activities
- Transition plans required for selected large borrowers

## Product Development
- Expand green financing and sustainability-linked products
- Provide customer education on sustainable choices

## Governance
- Sustainability oversight at executive and board level
- Annual target setting and progress reviews

## Disclosure
- Public reporting aligned to recognized frameworks
- Clear methodology for metrics and assumptions
