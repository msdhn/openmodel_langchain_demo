# KYC Policy

## Purpose
Establishes customer due diligence requirements to verify identity and prevent misuse of services.

## Requirements
- Collect and validate official identity documentation
- Verify address and beneficial ownership where applicable
- Risk-rate customer profiles at onboarding

## Ongoing Due Diligence
- Refresh KYC data based on risk tier
- Trigger enhanced checks on unusual behavior
- Keep customer records current and auditable

## Enhanced Due Diligence
- Required for high-risk customers or jurisdictions
- Additional source-of-funds/source-of-wealth checks

## Record Keeping
- Maintain records per legal retention requirements
- Secure access controls for sensitive data
