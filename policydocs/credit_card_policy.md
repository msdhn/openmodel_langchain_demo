# Credit Card Policy

## Purpose
Defines issuance, usage controls, fraud response, and billing standards for credit cards.

## Card Issuance
- Eligibility based on risk and affordability checks
- Credit limits assigned by risk tier
- Terms must be clearly communicated before activation

## Usage Controls
- Configurable transaction controls (online, international, contactless)
- Merchant category restrictions where required
- Real-time risk monitoring for unusual activity

## Billing and Interest
- Statement cycle and due date must be clearly disclosed
- Interest and fees applied per agreed product terms
- Late payment handling must follow fair collections guidelines

## Card Blocking and Fraud
- Customers can lock or block cards via official channels
- Suspected fraud must trigger immediate risk workflows
- Dispute and chargeback process must include tracking reference

## Customer Protection
- No disclosure of full PAN data in support channels
- Strong authentication required for sensitive actions
