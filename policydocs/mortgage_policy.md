# Mortgage Policy

## Purpose
This policy governs home loan origination, risk checks, and ongoing servicing.

## Scope
- Primary residence mortgages
- Refinancing applications
- Buy-to-let mortgages where permitted

## Key Requirements
- Property valuation by approved channels
- Verified customer affordability
- Source-of-funds verification for down payment

## Risk Controls
- Loan-to-value limits by risk segment
- Stress testing against rate increase scenarios
- Enhanced review for high-risk profiles

## Servicing Standards
- Monthly statements must be accurate and timely
- Escrow and tax handling must follow legal requirements
- Arrears management should prioritize sustainable repayment plans

## Foreclosure Principles
- Last-resort option after support pathways are exhausted
- Legal and customer notification timelines must be followed
