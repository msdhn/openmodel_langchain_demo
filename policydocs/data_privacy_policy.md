# Data Privacy Policy

## Purpose
Sets requirements for collection, use, retention, and protection of personal data.

## Data Handling Principles
- Collect only necessary data for defined purposes
- Use data lawfully, fairly, and transparently
- Restrict processing to approved use cases

## Customer Rights
- Access, correction, and deletion requests supported
- Objection and consent withdrawal processed per law

## Security and Access
- Encryption in transit and at rest for sensitive data
- Role-based access controls and least privilege
- Logging of sensitive data access events

## Retention
- Apply retention schedules by data category
- Securely dispose data after retention period

## Third Parties
- Data sharing only under approved agreements
- Vendor controls and periodic risk reviews required
