# Loan Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines eligibility, approval standards, and servicing rules for personal and business loans.

## Scope
- Personal term loans
- Small business loans
- Education support loans

## Eligibility
- Verified identity and address
- Stable income or business cash flow evidence
- Acceptable credit risk profile

## Underwriting Rules
- Debt-to-income ratio thresholds must be met
- Credit score is a key but not sole decision factor
- Affordability check is mandatory before approval

## Disbursement
- Funds are released only after final agreement acceptance
- Disbursement account must be customer-owned and verified

## Repayment and Collections
- Repayment schedule is fixed at origination unless formally restructured
- Grace periods and late fees follow product terms
- Hardship support can be offered after customer assessment

## Compliance
- Fair lending rules apply to all applicants
- Decisions must be explainable and auditable
