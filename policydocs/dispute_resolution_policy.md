# Dispute Resolution Policy

## Purpose
Standardizes handling of customer complaints, transaction disputes, and service grievances.

## Intake Channels
- Mobile app support
- Secure web support portal
- Contact center and branch submissions

## Case Handling
- Assign case ID and acknowledgment on receipt
- Categorize by issue type and urgency
- Track status updates until closure

## Timelines
- Initial response target within defined SLA
- Resolution target based on dispute category
- Delays require proactive customer notification

## Evidence and Decisioning
- Collect relevant transaction and communication records
- Apply policy and regulatory criteria consistently
- Communicate final decision with rationale

## Escalation
- Customers may request second-level review
- Regulatory escalation path communicated where applicable
