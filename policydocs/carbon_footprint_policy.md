# Carbon Footprint Policy

## Purpose
Defines measurement, reduction, and reporting standards for operational and financed carbon footprint.

## Measurement Scope
- Scope 1: direct emissions
- Scope 2: purchased energy emissions
- Scope 3: selected financed and supplier emissions

## Baseline and Targets
- Baseline year must be documented and fixed
- Annual reduction targets must be published internally
- Deviations require documented corrective plans

## Reduction Levers
- Energy efficiency and renewable sourcing
- Low-emission procurement standards
- Portfolio engagement for financed emissions reduction

## Data Quality
- Data sources must be traceable
- Estimation methods must be documented
- Independent review required for material metrics

## Reporting
- Internal quarterly updates
- External sustainability reporting cycle aligned with regulation
